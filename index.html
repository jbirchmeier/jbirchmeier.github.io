But, who is she?

Jenelle started her coding journey when she stopped feeling challenged and inspired at her job as a finance manager for a government agency. At first it was playing around on Codecademy but when public finance started to look less and less like a long-term career, she took it to the next level and enrolled in the Front End Developer Nanodegree by Udacity. The constant challenges, creative problem solving and the satisfaction of watching a website come to life inspired her to take the leap and move to switch careers. Her first class with Girl Develop It (Chicago chapter) confirmed that she made the right choice. After feeling like something of an outsider in her Government roles, she finally felt like she was among peers.
When she is not working or coding, you can usually find her nose in a book and a cat in her lap. On weekends you will find her at a local music venue screaming in the crowds and dancing to some indie-rock or riot girrrrl band. To give back to her community, Jenelle volunteers with Coderdojo Chicago and the Illinois Safe Schools Alliance.
